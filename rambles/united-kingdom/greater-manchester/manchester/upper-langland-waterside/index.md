A good place for a stroll if you’re in the area. A nice little escape from the hustle and bustle of the nearby motorway and Trafford Centre. Upper Langland Waterside is a peaceful path running alongside the Manchester Ship Canal, perfect for a walk or a bit of fresh air. While there’s not much to see apart from plenty of ducks and geese, it’s a pleasant spot if you just want to unwind.
