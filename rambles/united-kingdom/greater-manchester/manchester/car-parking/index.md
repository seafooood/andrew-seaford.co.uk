The car park is a good place to park if you're looking to explore the River Mersey or walk across Simons Bridge. It's a free car park with about 10 spaces, so it's a good option if you can find a spot.
​Just a heads-up, the road leading to the car park can be very muddy, so it's best to drive with caution. There are also multiple signs warning about the area being a flood risk, so it's worth bearing that in mind, especially during bad weather. Park at your own risk.
