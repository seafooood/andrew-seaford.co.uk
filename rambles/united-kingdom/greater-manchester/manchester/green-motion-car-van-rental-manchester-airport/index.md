The staff were supper helpful especially the manager and Paul in the car park.
The Green Motion office is located in the Regus building opposite the airport. You can call the office on 0161 260 0420 (select option 1) for shuttle pickup. The shuttle typically runs every 30 minutes and picks up from Terminal 1: Bus Stop E Terminal 2: Bus Stop F Terminal 3: Bus Stop C Look for a white 17-seater minibus with Green Motion signage displayed on the front windscreen.
