Errwood Hall Car Park is a fantastic starting point for exploring the stunning Goyt Valley. From here, you can easily access walks to iconic landmarks like the ruins of Errwood Hall and Errwood Reservoir. The car park is part of the Goyt Valley Traffic Scheme, so be mindful of the road closures on Sundays and Bank Holidays (11:00 am - 4:00 pm) from May 1st to September 30th.
