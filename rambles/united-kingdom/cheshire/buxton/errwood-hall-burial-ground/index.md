Errwood Hall Burial Ground offers a deeply peaceful and historically significant experience, nestled within the truly beautiful landscape of the Goyt Valley. Located just behind the ruins of Errwood Hall, the burial ground is easily accessible. The closest parking is at Errwood Hall Parking, from which it's a pleasant walk through the scenic surroundings.
