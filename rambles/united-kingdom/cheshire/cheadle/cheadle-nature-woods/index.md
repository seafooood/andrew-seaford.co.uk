A peaceful little spot tucked away in Cheadle. Micker Brook runs gently through the woods, adding a nice touch to the surroundings. There are two large grassy areas and a few trees, making it a decent place for a quiet walk. There’s not a huge amount to see but it’s still a calm escape from the nearby roads.
