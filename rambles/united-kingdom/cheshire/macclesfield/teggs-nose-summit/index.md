Tegg’s Nose summit is a fantastic mountain summit in Tegg’s Nose Country Park, standing at 382 metres. The summit is marked by a large walk-in view indicator, which highlights key landmarks in the surrounding landscape.

On a clear day, you can enjoy spectacular views of:

Kinder Scout

Macclesfield Forest

Shutlingsloe

Bosley Minn

Mow Cop

Macclesfield Canal

Beeston Castle

Jodrell Bank

Alderley Edge
