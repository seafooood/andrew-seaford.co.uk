The car park for the play ground is next to the village hall. The entrance to the park is behind the scout building. The open play area is recommended for children of 6-12 years. The enclosed play area is recommended for children of 1-6 years. The park has swing, slide, zip line, table tennis, basketball net, football pitch, tennis court, and picnic benches.
