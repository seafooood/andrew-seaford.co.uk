The Danes Moss Tramway was a horse drawn tramway built by the British Moss Litter Company to transport peat from the moss. It remained in use until the mid-1960s. Unfortunately, there isn’t much left to see today, just a few rails and some decayed wooden sleepers. However, for those interested in local history or industrial heritage, it’s still a fascinating place to explore.
