The text on the memorial stone says:

**Here John Turner was cast  away in heavy snow storm in the night in or about the year 1755. The print of a womans shoe was found by his side in the snow were he lay dead.**

The stone is on Ewrin Lane by the side of the road. There is no were to stop on the narrow lane.
