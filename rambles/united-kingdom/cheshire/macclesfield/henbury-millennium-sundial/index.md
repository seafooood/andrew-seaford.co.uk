The Henbury Millennium Sundial is located at the far end of the Henbury Millennium Green, across from St. Thomas Church. The sundial is approximately 12 feet in diameter. In April 2023, a plaque was installed on the sundial to commemorate the 100th birthday of Sir Francis Graham-Smith, who was a Henbury Millennium Green Trustee and Henbury Parish Councillor. Serving as the thirteenth Astronomer Royal, he was responsible for designing and installing the sundial.
