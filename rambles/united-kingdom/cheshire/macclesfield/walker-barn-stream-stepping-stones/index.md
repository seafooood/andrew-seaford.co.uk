The stepping stones cross Walker Barn Stream, which flows into Tegg’s Nose Reservoir. Located near Tegg’s Nose Country Park, they offer a charming spot for walkers to pause and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

The stepping stones are just a short walk from both Tegg’s Nose Country Park car park and Tegg’s Nose Reservoir car park, making them easily accessible for visitors exploring the area.
