## West Park Castle

The Castle is a short walk from the West Park Cafe and Playground.
[West Park Castle](west-park-castle/index.md)

## Church Lane Play Area

The open play area is recommended for children of 6-12 years. The enclosed play area is recommended for children of 1-6 years. The park has swing, slide, zip line, table tennis, basketball net, football pitch, tennis court, and picnic benches.
[Church Lane Play Area](church-lane-play-area/index.md)

## Adlington Road play area

The Happy Valley Paly Area has a swing, slide, climbing frame, basketball court, and a skateboard park.
[text](adlington-road-play-area/index.md)

## Lanark Walk play area

Lanark park has a good variety of equipment including swings, a slide, a roundabout, BMX track, basketball hoop and a football pitch.
[Lanark Walk play area](lanark-walk-play-area/index.md)

## Henbury Millennium Green

A small playground, swing, picnic table and a large sundial.
[Henbury Millennium Green](the-nose-coffee/index.md)

## Franklin Close play area

Small playground great for young kids. The play area has swings, climbing frame, slide, fireman pole, seesaw.
[Franklin Close play area](franklin-close-play-area/index.md)

## Prestbury Park

The park has a large football pitch.
[Prestbury Park](prestbury-park/index.md)

## Bodmin Avenue play area

A little playground perfect for younger children. It has swings, a slide, and a climbing frame. The park can be accessed from Bodmin Avenue (near number 30), Madron Avenue (near number 28), or Polperro Close (near number 14).
[Bodmin Avenue play area](bodmin-avenue-play-area/index.md)

## The Villas play area

A lovely little playground perfect for younger children. It has swing, a slide, seesaw and a climbing frame.
[The Villas play area](the-villas-play-area/index.md)

## Lowerhouse Road Playground

A small Playground for infants and toddlers well-equipped with swings, a slide, a roundabout, a seesaw, and a cute train-shaped climbing frame.
