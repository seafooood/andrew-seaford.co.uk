A truly stunning and unique waterfall. It's an amazing sight to see the water cascading directly from the clifftop down onto the beach. The walk to get there was a fantastic adventure in itself. We started at Watchet Harbour Marine, went down the Helwell Bay Beach Stairs, and walked along Helwell and Doniford beaches to reach it. The walk back along the coastal path completed a perfect circular route with beautiful views all the way. Highly recommended.
