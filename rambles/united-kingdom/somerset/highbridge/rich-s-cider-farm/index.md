Rich's Cider Farm offers an impressive selection of ciders, ranging from dry to sweet, along with other local produce. One of the most fascinating aspects is seeing the sheer scale of their operation. They boast an incredible 10,000-gallon vat, which holds an astounding 80,000 pints of cider! To put that into perspective, if you were to drink 4 pints a day, it would take you an astonishing 54 years, 9 months, and 20 days to finish it all. It's a fun fact that really highlights the farm's heritage and passion for cider making.
