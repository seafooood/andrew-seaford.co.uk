Selworth Church is a wonderful grade one listed building. The sign outside says "It is almost always open". There is a small car park at the front of the church. Parking is by donation, which goes towards the upkeep. It's the perfect starting point, as it's just a short and pleasant walk to the Periwinkle Cottage Tea Room and the rest of the lovely village of beautiful thatched cottages.
