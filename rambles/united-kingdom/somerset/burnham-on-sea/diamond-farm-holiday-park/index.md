We had a wonderfully relaxing night camping at Diamond Farm. Our spot overlooked the River Axe, right next to the Bird Screen created by Katy Hallett. It was fascinating to see how the screen, which looks like piles of driftwood, protects the roosting bird life from the nearby path.
