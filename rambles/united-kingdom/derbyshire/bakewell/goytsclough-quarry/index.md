Goyts Clough Quarry is an excellent starting point for anyone looking to explore the beautiful Goyt Valley and its surrounding attractions. This free layby offers convenient access to a variety of scenic walks, including routes to the iconic Cat and Fiddle, the quarry itself, and the atmospheric ruins of Errwood Hall. The stunning natural beauty of the Goyt Valley is truly on display from this location.

Access to the layby is straightforward via a one-way road that leads from Errwood Hall, heading towards Derbyshire Bridge Car Park and the Cat and Fiddle. It's an ideal spot for adventurers and nature lovers alike to begin their journey into this picturesque part of the Peak District.
